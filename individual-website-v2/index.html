Welcome

Thank you for visiting our site! We breed registered Kinder Goats in Alexander, Arkansas. We love goats and are excited to have the opportunity to share our passion with you! Whether you are new to goats or have kept them for many years, we hope you will find this site to be a valuable resource. Please check in often to see what's new, or contact us if there is any way we can help you!

-Anya & Matt Littlefield

Current Goals

  • Improve overall quality of herd through selective breeding
  • Bring in buck with new bloodlines and great conformation
  • Grow doe herd
  • Sell adult does that do not make the cut
  • Keep all paperwork current through KGBA

Future Goals

  • Breed for first generation Kinders using registered Pygmy x Nubian parents
  • Sign up for milk test program to obtain milk stars for does
  • Participate in KGBA sanctioned show
  • Expand pasture area

Resources

For more information about Kinder goats or to become a member, visit the Kinder Goat Breeders Association website.

To network with other goat folks or ask questions, check out the Kinder Goat Folks Facebook group!
